Udacious Tomatoes: A Selection of Movies Worth Watching

HyperNormalisation

Documentary by Adam Curtis (2016)

Thought-provoking BBC documentary on power and narratives in contemporary society.

Samsara

Documentary by Rob Fricke (2011)

Visually stunning meditation on humankind's simultaneous majesty and vulnerability.

Millennium Mambo

Drama by Hou Hsiao-Hsien (2001)

Taiwanese art house film about the fleeting beauty of youth.

Short Term 12

Drama by Destin Daniel Cretton (2013)

Heart-breaking story of a foster home's employees and residents.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Drama by Michel Gondry (2004)

Is love worth it? Who knows???

My Neighbor Totoro

Animation by Hayao Miyazaki (1988)

Adorable, feel-good cartoon about siblings in the Japanese countryside and their mysterious friend.
